Great work speaks with purpose

Design is more than what you see—it’s how it works, feels, and connects.

Understated by choice, purposeful by design

I approach challenges with intention—balancing bold ideas with subtle details and bringing clarity and care to every project. Whether collaborating with teams or individuals, I focus on uncovering opportunities, refining solutions, and delivering work that’s as human as it is polished.

The best ideas—and people—don’t need to shout. They listen, connect, and leave their mark.

Great work happens with great people

I’ve worked with global brands, category leaders, and independent visionaries across industries. I’m most drawn to those shaping how people enjoy the world.

Meta

Meta develops global products that connect communities and shape the future of technology. I wrote conceptual copy that shaped this confidential product’s design direction and informed its future expression across industrial and brand applications.

IBM Watson Health

IBM Watson Health delivers AI-driven healthcare innovations. I designed digital storytelling and trade show experiences to educate and engage healthcare audiences.

Google

Google’s Fireside Sessions convene thought leaders from around the world. I led design for the campaign identity, motion storyboards, and supporting digital visuals. Collaborators: K. Burford, M. Oh

Harley-Davidson

Harley-Davidson Museum brings the brand’s spirit to a hospitality setting at Motor restaurant. I directed design for print and digital materials, including a custom beer and brewery book. Collaborators: M. Oh

Four Seasons Hotels and Resorts

Four Seasons Hotels and Resorts defines global luxury hospitality. I designed print materials to enhance guest experiences at several Four Seasons properties.

O’Hare International Airport

O’Hare International Airport is a landmark of global travel. I designed the 50th anniversary identity, drawing from the airport’s visual history to inspire a forward-looking celebration. Collaborators: L. Galambos

United Airlines

United Airlines leads global aviation innovation. I designed custom iconography for in-flight service apps.

BP

BP’s Whiting Refinery modernization advanced energy production in the Midwest. I designed corporate publications and a print newsletter to communicate this story. Collaborators: L. Galambos
